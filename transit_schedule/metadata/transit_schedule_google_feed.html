Section 1 Overview
Originator Metropolitan Council - Metro Transit
Title Metro Transit Schedule Data - General Transit Feed Specification (GTFS)
Abstract The Metro Transit schedule data includes bus and LRT schedules for the Twin Cities metropolitan area. This includes data for all public transit companies in the Minneapolis / St. Paul metropolitan area except the Minnesota Valley Transit Authority. The data uses the General Transit Feed Specification. This specification can be found at https://developers.google.com/transit/gtfs/reference?csw=1.
Purpose To provide schedule data for public transit services in the Minneapolis / St. Paul metropolitan area.
